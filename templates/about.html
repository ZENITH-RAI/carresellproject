About this project

AutoValue is a full-stack predictive tool for getting a realistic resale price for a used car. Tell us about the vehicle—make, age, mileage, and a few specs—and we return a precise estimate, a realistic price range, and similar market alternatives informed by historical data.

Why it exists

Buying or selling a used car often comes down to guesswork. This project was built as coursework to explore how data engineering and machine learning can support a clearer starting point: not just a single guaranteed number, but a reasoned estimated range you can actively compare with our recommended market listings and your own judgment.

Core Features

  • Smart Price Ranges: Instead of relying on a single prediction, we calculate a minimum and maximum estimated value based on the model's Mean Absolute Error (MAE).
  • Market Alternatives: Our recommendation engine automatically scans historical datasets to find the top 5 most similar cars, allowing users to compare predicted values against real-world market prices.
  • Model Transparency: We believe in transparent AI. A dedicated Machine Learning metrics dashboard allows you to view the algorithm's performance (RMSE, R² Score) and feature weight importance in real-time.

How it works

Under the hood, a custom Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD) Regressor learns from historical sales data. Before predictions are made, our robust data pipeline engineers new features (like age, power-to-weight ratios, and luxury brand categorization) and normalizes the inputs. When you submit the form, the model outputs a prediction, while a parallel similarity-scoring algorithm calculates Euclidean-like distances to fetch the closest real-world car matches.

A note on accuracy

Real prices depend on condition, service history, location, negotiation, and timing. Treat any number or range here as a data-driven guide, not financial or legal advice.
